About Tim Downey

Based in beautiful Hobart, Tasmania, I'm an amateur photographer with a deep passion for photography across all genres. Armed with my Sony A7RIV and favorite lenses including the Sigma 85mm DG DN and Sony 50mm, I capture moments that matter.

From intimate portraits using my trusted 50mm lens to dynamic workshop photography, I bring enthusiasm and technical skill to every shoot. My work spans diverse subjects and styles, always driven by my love for the craft.
